Frequently Asked Questions

Please read through these common questions before contacting support.

Q: Are tickets sent to my postal address?



A: No, tickets are never posted to our customers. All tickets are stored online, tickets are sent to your email address and can also be found in account management.

Q: Do you accept any other payment methods such as EFT or paypal?



A: Currently, we are limited to accepting all forms of credit and debit card paymenst. However, we will be adding more payment methods in the future.

Q: I can not login into my account?



A: Please make sure your caps lock is off on your keyboard as the password is case sensitive.

Q: Will there be any vegetarian food at the event?



A: Yes!, we have made sure to provide a wide variety of foods. If you need help find specific food please loacate your nearest help worker on the day of the event.

Q: Are you only based in South Africa?



A: Yes, currently we are restricted to South Africa because of the covid-19 outbreak. We have plans to take ENCRYPTED overseas in the near future, please follow our socials to stay up to date on any developments.
