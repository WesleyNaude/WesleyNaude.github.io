About

Encrypted Africa is the biggest music concert in the Southern Hemisphere. We bring together artists from around the world to hold the performances of their lives. This year Encrypted will be held on the 04 June in Johannesburg and 31 June in Cape Town. A regular tickets costs R850 and a full access ticket costs R2000, a small price to pay for an experience of your lifetime.


Our story

Encrypted Africa started out as a group of guys aspiring to create a brand that strives to create unforgettable, life long and life changing experiences. They started hosting small events in small resturants around their home city Johannesburg, 10 years later their brand Encrypted Africa has become the most prestigous event in the whole southern hemisphere.
