Facilities

Having trouble finding the essentials?

Parking

ENCRYPTED AFRICA parking is available on the day of the event from 5am-12pm.

The parking fee is R10, payable at the vending machines located at the entrance and prior to returning to the parkade at the end of your visit.

There is direct access to the event from the parkade by following the clearly marked trails.

Please note that this parkade is not available prior to 5am and our parking is unfortunately extremely limited. If you are have any questions or concerns, our security team on duty will assist in every way possible.

Toilets

Toilets can be found both inside and outside the venue, they are clearly marked. The Toilets are located to the right of the the main stage(Stage 1 ). If you are still struggling to find them, please ask your nearest help worker for help.

Help workers

Our help workers have been trained extensively and are a reliable source for any help you may need. They can be located at the front entrance and around the venue, they wear a bright yellow shirt marked “Help worker”.
